FAQ

  • How often should I wash my hands?
    You should wash your hands regularly, especially before meals, after using the restroom, and after touching potentially contaminated surfaces.
  • What is the importance of sanitation?
    Sanitation is essential to prevent the spread of disease by ensuring clean water and reducing waste buildup.
  • How can I promote hygiene in my community?
    You can promote hygiene by educating others on proper practices and ensuring public spaces have sanitation facilities.
  • What should I do if I feel sick?
    If you feel sick, rest and monitor symptoms, stay hydrated, and seek medical attention if necessary.
  • How does personal hygiene impact mental health?
    Good personal hygiene can boost self-esteem, reduce stress, and positively impact mental well-being.
